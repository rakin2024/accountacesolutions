Payroll Management

Single Touch Payroll (STP) – The introduction of STP provides a great incentive for organizations to look at and improve internal processes and potentially review their payroll software for a more compliant reporting model. Greater efficiency through better digital payroll processes and solutions can only be better for your business. Small Business Superannuation Clearing House (SBSCH): The Small Business Superannuation Clearing House (SBSCH) is a service you can use to make super guarantee (SG) contributions. Eligible businesses are those with 19 or fewer employees or an annual aggregated turnover of less than $10 million. Small Business Superannuation Clearing House If you’re looking to streamline your payroll process, you may want to look into superannuation and payroll management services. These programs can help you automate many processes and keep track of all your employee data. They also can automate various aspects of your payroll, from setting up a timesheet to setting up payments for employees. In addition to these features, you can also create reports that help you monitor your company’s compliance with state and federal regulations.
