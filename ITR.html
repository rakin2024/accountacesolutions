We offer personalized and transparent tax return assistance, with a focus on delivering a high standard of advice to self-employed and professional individuals. Filing an Individual Tax Return is a very important part of the federal taxing system in the United States. Whether you’re self-employed or employed by a business, you’ll need to file an Individual Income Tax Return every year.
