ATO Representation

With our years of experience dealing with the Australian Taxation Office, we can ensure our clients are being properly represented before the tax regulators. ATO Representation is a service that provides consultation and support services to Australian businesses. This is particularly important in a time of economic uncertainty, and this service is essential to the survival of Australian businesses. ATO representatives have a wealth of experience representing Australian businesses before the ATO. The benefits of ATO Representation are numerous, and their services range from tax compliance to business advice. The following is a summary of what they have to offer: ATO Stewardship Groups focus on a specific product segment, system, or market. This arrangement is generally put in place when the complexity of a program is high. In addition to consultation, ATO Representation provides assistance in the area of risk management, and members share ideas from recent GST workshops. Core design teams are based on the objectives of a program and are generally put in place when uncertainty is high..
